A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, (born October 15, 1931, Rameswaram, India—died July 27, 2015, Shillong), Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs.When Kalam was 5 years old, he had a teacher Muthu Iyer. He took a special interest in Kalam because he performed well in class. Iyer played a crucial role in nurturing good habits in Kalam
